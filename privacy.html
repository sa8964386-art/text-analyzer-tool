Privacy Policy & About Us

About Smart Text Analyzer

Smart Text Analyzer is a free, web-based utility designed to help content creators, writers, SEO specialists, and developers instantly calculate text metrics. Our tool provides real-time word counting, character tracking, reading time estimation, and text case conversions to streamline your writing workflow.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. It is Smart Text Analyzer's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our website.

Data Processing & Security

All text processed using Smart Text Analyzer is handled locally within your web browser (client-side). We do not store, save, transmitted, or collect any text or content you paste into our text editor.

Log Files & Analytics

Like most standard websites, we may collect non-personally identifying information that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request.

Cookies and Web Beacons

We use cookies to store information about visitors' preferences and optimize user experience. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies (such as the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on user visits to this website and other websites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.
